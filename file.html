About me

I'm Barakah Ademola-Majekodunmi

As a photographer, my journey began in the unassuming setting of my high school's photography club. It was there, amidst the whirlwind of adolescence, that I discovered my innate passion for capturing the essence of life through the lens. Fueled by this early fascination, I embarked on a rigorous educational path, earning a Bachelor's, Master's, and ultimately a Ph.D. in Photography. These years of academic pursuit were marked by an insatiable thirst for knowledge, a relentless dedication to perfecting my craft, and a profound appreciation for the art form's ability to transcend boundaries and evoke emotions. Today, armed with a wealth of experience and expertise, I am committed to capturing moments of beauty, truth, and humanity, and sharing them with the world through my unique perspective as, a photographer with a passion for storytelling.
